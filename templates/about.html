About RIVIÈRE RV PARK

Your premier riverfront RV camping destination

Our Story

Welcome to RIVIÈRE RV PARK, where adventure meets comfort on the beautiful riverfront. Our unique location features what's called a levee - a very large hill that provides stunning views and a distinctive entrance to our park.

We understand that accessing our park requires following specific directions to safely navigate the levee with your RV or camper. This special approach ensures the safety and enjoyment of all our guests.

Our park offers modern amenities including bathhouses with walk-in showers, a pavilion for events and rentals, full hookups, and on-site laundry facilities. Choose from three scenic location options: Riverfront sites with direct water access, Riverview sites with beautiful water vistas, or peaceful Pond sites.

Whether you're launching your boat from our on-site ramp (with a larger public landing just 1 mile away), fishing in our well-stocked ponds, or relaxing by your campfire, RIVIÈRE RV PARK is your home away from home.

Our Amenities

Everything you need for a comfortable stay

Laundromat Building

Our modern laundromat building is video monitored 24/7 for your safety, and features convenient parking for easy access.

Walk-In Bathhouse

Our bathrooms are equipped with large walk-in showers and endless hot water for your comfort.

Event Pavilion

A pavilion available to rent for special events, and where we host community activities. Check our calendar for upcoming events!

Boat Launching

Smaller boat launch on site with large public landing approximately 1 mile away for your boating convenience.

Fishing Ponds

Our two fishing ponds are always kept well stocked and fishing is free to all campers. A fence is installed around the pond for child safety.

Fire Pits and Tables

Each site has a fire pit, picnic table, and water hose so that you can enjoy the outdoors in comfort.

Spacious RV Sites

RV sites are spaced out through the park so that you won't feel cramped by your neighbor. Enjoy privacy and comfort.

Shade Trees

The park is shaded by many beautiful trees providing a forest getaway atmosphere and natural cooling.

Full Hookups

All sites feature full hookups including water, electric, and sewer connections for your convenience.
